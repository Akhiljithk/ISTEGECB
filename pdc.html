Project development challenge

JOIN PDC 2022-23

ISTE GECBH conducts the Project Development Challenge (PDC) every year- a month long event that upholds our reputation as the only technical society in college that provides hands-on Project development experience. ISTE GECB's very own Robotics Club- Bartonoidz organizes the event, providing components and assistance for developing projects in all domains irrespective of members' prior knowledge or experience in the field.
With the right guidance and mentorship, the PDC channels students' fascination for technology into working models of robotics and engineering craftsmanship, letting beginners develop and take pride in their own projects!
