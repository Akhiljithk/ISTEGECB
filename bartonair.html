ABOUT US

The lure of flying is the lure of beauty. ISTE SC GECB also has a space for students with dream of exploring the unbounded skies. Bartonair, a club for all things that fly, a community that builds, discusses and fly their creations. The purpose of the club is to promote safe, enjoyable flying and aviation-related activities, and to provide the opportunity for its members to fly aircraft economically and conveniently under ISTE Students Chapter GEC Barton Hill. We promote the joy of flying to the community by being an organization with a special focus on safety and proficiency. At Bartonair we inspire each other to design and build machines that could fly.
