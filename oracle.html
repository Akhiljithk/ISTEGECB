The Oracle

Oracle,an online inquirer newsletter released every month features students' queries to accomplished personalities, institutes or organisations. Previous editions were of prominent personalities like Dr E Sreedharan - The Metroman, Dr P M Ajayan in field of nanotechnology, Prof V K Damodaran and many more.The basic idea behind this newsletter is to provide our student chapter members with the academic knowledge and they can remain updated with the monthly current affairs through different sections of this newsletter. Sections like games, fun facts and puzzles have been added for fun.
