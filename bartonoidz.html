ABOUT US

Bartonoidz is a club for dedicated enthusiast in the field of robotics. Inaugurated on 7th April 2017 by the then Principal of the college Dr Vijayakumar, Bartonoidz is an inevitable part of ISTE SC GECB. The club never failed to meet the expectation of its passionate members. With strength of 250 members we assemble, create and discover the world of robots. The main objective of the club is to promote the interest of the students in assembling robots and provide them with the facilities to achieve their dream.
